Malouf Companies™

Malouf Companies™ spans 19 years, six states and multiple industries. Headquartered in the largely rural Cache Valley, Malouf™ brings high-quality jobs and economic stimulus to northern Utah, employing over 1,000 people and operating in three corporate offices and seven distribution centers.

We are lifestyle and wellness experts. Our core business is home furnishings, but we’ve grown into six key categories (consumer products, services, technology, retail, logistics, and venture) through a suite of nationally recognized brands and powerful partnerships.

Our mission is clear: Redefine business.
Welcome to our corporate evolution—fueled by values and strengthened by people.

Expertise

For 19 years, we’ve been building our expertise. The resulting business moves quickly, adapts to market demands, and capitalizes on consumer trends. Our innovations and history of disrupting made us a leading provider of high-quality home furnishings at every price point.

Malouf Foundation

Formalized in 2016 as our charity arm, the Malouf Foundation™ is a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit dedicated to confronting child sexual exploitation, specifically sex trafficking and online abuse. The Foundation fulfills its mission by providing education, promoting healing, and furthering advocacy for survivors. In 2022, the Elizabeth Smart Foundation officially became a part of the Malouf Foundation™ to broaden the impact of both organizations.

Certified B Corporation®

We are using business as a force for good. Since 2019, we have proudly been a B Corp™. We meet the highest standards of verified social and environmental performance, transparency, and accountability.
